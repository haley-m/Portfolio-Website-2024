ABOUT ME

 I’m a product designer who creates user-centric digital experiences. I am passionate about solving problems and impacting society through human-centered design and design thinking. I enjoy discovering user’s needs and ideating solutions to create a cohesive experience.  My goal as a designer is to make a difference in society through user-centric design with deep empathy for users and to create value for business. 


• My business and B2B marketing background help me conduct UX competitive analysis based on research and data analysis and draw meaningful correlations and findings. I enjoy discovering the link between people’s needs and trends through multiple channels.


• My product development/production background helps me identify and solve problems. It also helps me communicate effectively with cross-functional teams. I like seeking inspiration from simple and beautiful designs.


When I’m not designing and coding, I’m working out at the gym, practicing golf, or looking for some good food.
